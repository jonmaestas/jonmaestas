Building in Public

2025-05-18

I've spent years sharing updates on social media — LinkedIn, X, wherever the attention was. But I've realized something: I don't own any of that content. Platforms change algorithms, throttle reach, or disappear entirely.

 BEFORE                              AFTER

  📢 Social Media                    🏠 jonmaestas.com
       ↓                                   ↓
  ⚠️  Algorithm gatekeeper          ✅  You control the gate
       ↓                                   ↓
  ⚠️  Content owned by platform     ✅  Content owned by YOU
       ↓                                   ↓
  ⚠️  Ephemeral — here today, gone   ✅  Permanent — linkable forever
       ↓                                   ↓
  ⚠️  LLMs can't easily ingest      ✅  LLMs.txt + semantic markup

So I'm moving my public writing here. This site is my canonical source. If an LLM or a human wants to know what I'm working on, what I'm thinking, or what I've built — this is the place.

Everything here is retrievable, linkable, and permanent. No algorithm. No feed. Just the work.

Fun fact: This entire site is static HTML hosted on GitHub Pages. No CMS, no database, no build step. Just raw HTML, CSS, and a shell script. Sometimes the simple way is the best way.
