Training Sessions: Moving from Rep-by-Rep to Full-Session Intelligence

2026-07-09

New flow: record a full session. The app identifies every rep automatically. From there you can save the good ones, compare any two side-by-side, ask questions about the entire session, or pull consistent, coach-level feedback on any individual rep and the full session at once.

That last part is the real unlock. You get the same quality of analysis whether you’re looking at one rep or the whole workout.

The technical change that made this possible: we switched from Gemini to TwelveLabs Pegasus in June 2026.

Gemini

  • No real rep identification in longer videos
  • Struggled with consistency on individual rep analysis
  • Frame-by-frame workarounds

TwelveLabs Pegasus

  • Native video model, purpose-built for session-level breakdowns
  • Coach-level accuracy on reps
  • More consistent, faster, and cheaper

Side-by-side testing on individual reps showed Pegasus matched coach-level accuracy while delivering more consistent feedback. It was faster and cheaper too.

Feedback is now much more consistent, concise, and faster across the board. The session layer only became possible because Pegasus understands video natively.
