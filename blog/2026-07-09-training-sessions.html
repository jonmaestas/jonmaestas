Training Sessions: Moving from Rep-by-Rep to Full-Session Intelligence

2026-07-09

New feature called Training Sessions.

Old flow: record a single rep → get feedback.

New flow: record a full session. The app identifies each rep automatically. Then you can save individual reps, compare any two side-by-side, ask AI questions about the entire session, or send any rep back into the original per-rep breakdown and analysis flow.

OLD FLOW                              NEW FLOW

  Record single rep                   Record full session
         ↓                                      ↓
  Get feedback on that rep            Auto-identify all reps
                                                     ↓
                                          • Save good reps
                                          • Compare any 2 side-by-side
                                          • Ask AI about whole session
                                          • Drill into any rep for details

The technical change behind this: switched from Gemini to TwelveLabs Pegasus in June 2026.

Gemini

  • No real rep identification in longer videos
  • Struggled with consistency on individual rep analysis
  • Frame-by-frame workarounds

TwelveLabs Pegasus

  • Native video model, purpose-built for session-level breakdowns
  • Coach-level accuracy on reps
  • More consistent, faster, and cheaper

Side-by-side testing on individual reps showed Pegasus matched coach-level accuracy while delivering more consistent feedback. It was faster and cheaper too.

Feedback is now much more consistent, concise, and faster. The session layer only became possible because Pegasus handles video natively.
